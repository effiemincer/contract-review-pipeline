Each party retains all right, title, and interest in and to its pre-existing intellectual property. Any intellectual property created jointly by the parties during the performance of this Agreement shall be jointly owned, and neither party shall be required to account to the other for any profits derived from the use of such jointly owned intellectual property. Any work product created solely by one party's personnel and not derived from the other party's confidential information shall remain the sole property of the creating party. Where work is performed on a work-for-hire basis, the Agreement shall expressly state the assignment of rights and provide for fair compensation.
