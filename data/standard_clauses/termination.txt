Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice to the other party. Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice thereof, or (b) becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases to operate in the ordinary course of business. Upon termination, all rights and obligations shall cease except those that by their nature are intended to survive, including confidentiality, limitation of liability, and any accrued payment obligations.
