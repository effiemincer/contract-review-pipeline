All invoices shall be due and payable within thirty (30) days of the invoice date ("Net 30"). Late payments shall accrue interest at the lesser of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law. Either party may dispute an invoice in good faith by providing written notice within fifteen (15) days of receipt, specifying the nature of the dispute. Undisputed amounts must still be paid by the due date. No services shall be suspended for disputed amounts while the dispute resolution process is pending, provided the disputing party is acting in good faith.
